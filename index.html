About Me

Ike Bischof

I code with Javascript to make web apps and web games that explore a variety of creative ideas involving math, computer science, game design, and other topics. These projects are an opportunity to learn and discuss concepts like information theory, music theory, or game development, which I do here and on my YouTube channel, Ikebot.

Many of my projects are made with a popular Javascript library called P5JS, which is designed for creative coding and building generative and interactive art.

I'm currently a senior at Sacramento State University. I live in Roseville, California.
